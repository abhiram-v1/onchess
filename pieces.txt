## Usage Instructions:
- All pieces are in SVG format for crisp scaling
- White pieces are uppercase (K, Q, R, B, N, P)
- Black pieces are lowercase (k, q, r, b, n, p)
- Glothic pieces are local files, others are from Lichess repository
- Each style maintains clear distinction and recognition
- Perfect for different board themes and preferences

## Integration Notes:
- Glothic style uses local paths from Glothic style folder
- Other styles use reliable Lichess GitHub URLs
- Test piece visibility on different board themes
- Consider piece contrast with board colors
- Maintain consistent sizing across all pieces
- Verify piece recognition for accessibility
- All external URLs are from trusted open-source repositories
