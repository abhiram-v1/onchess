## Usage Instructions:
- Each theme includes a name and two color codes or texture files (Light and Dark squares)
- Light color/texture is for light squares (traditionally white)
- Dark color/texture is for dark squares (traditionally black)
- Colors are in hexadecimal format (#RRGGBB) or SVG file path
- Themes are categorized for easy selection
- All themes maintain good contrast for visibility
